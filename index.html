Sunshine Success Stories

Construction

Buildertrend Payroll Automation

Reed Design Build, LLC

The Challenge:

Manual timecard extraction from Buildertrend was eating up hours every pay period, with complex overtime calculations and job cost tracking making payroll a nightmare.

The Sunnyside Solution:

Built a comprehensive automation system using Axiom.ai for browser automation and Excel with Power Query for intelligent payroll processing. The system automatically extracts timecards, calculates overtime by job, and separates cost codes.

Before: 8+ hours per payroll
After: 30 minutes
"What used to take me all day now runs while I grab coffee. It's like magic!"
Finance

MATCHBIRD Receipt Matcher

Multiple Small Businesses

The Challenge:

Matching credit card statements to receipts and job codes was a tedious monthly task, with transactions often lacking clear descriptions and receipts piling up.

The Sunnyside Solution:

Developed MATCHBIRD, a Python application that automatically matches credit card transactions with receipts using OCR technology and intelligent matching algorithms. Seamlessly exports to QuickBooks-ready format.

Before: 2 days monthly reconciliation
After: 2 hours with 95% accuracy
"I was almost a year behind on reconciliation. MATCHBIRD caught me up in a week!"
Accounting

QuickBooks Transaction Processor

Construction & Service Companies

The Challenge:

A year's worth of unmatched transactions in QuickBooks Online, with complex job costing requirements and multiple approval workflows slowing everything down.

The Sunnyside Solution:

Created a FastAPI application with OAuth 2.0 integration for secure QuickBooks access. Built intelligent matching algorithms that process transactions in bulk while maintaining accuracy and audit trails.

Before: 12 months backlog
After: Caught up in 3 days
"I can finally see our real financial position. This tool saved our tax season!"

Excel Dashboard Intelligence
